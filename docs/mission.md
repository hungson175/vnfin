# vnfin-oss Mission

`vnfin-oss` is a clean-room rewrite of an open-source Python financial-data library.

## Audience

1. Long-term investors who need stable company, market, portfolio, valuation, and fundamentals data.
2. Macroeconomic analysts who need country/market/indicator time series and consistent schemas.
3. Developers building financial-advisor tools who need predictable APIs for downstream apps and AI agents.

## Product principles

- Clean-room design and implementation.
- Avoid VNStock/vnstock completely in research and implementation.
- Prefer official, lawful, license-clear data sources.
- Keep APIs simple, typed, documented, and stable.
- Prioritize long-term investing and macro analysis over short-term trading automation.
- Record provenance and terms for every source.
